How to Use
Four Phases. One Continuum.

The toolbox is organized to follow the actual sequence of museum development. Start with Phase 01 — Institutional Planning — before a single line is drawn. Bad feasibility is still fatal.

Who It's For
Founders. Directors. Boards. Staff.

Whether you are planning a new institution, evaluating an existing one, or preparing a capital campaign, the tools on this page give you the language and structure to move forward.

How the MVI Works

Every metric is expressed as a ratio or rate — never a raw number. Five years of IRS Form 990 filings, TripAdvisor and Google review data, Instagram followers normalized by operating budget, U.S. Census catchment demographics, and institutional history.

The use of public 990 data is deliberate. An analyst who knows how to read a 990 can reconstruct an institution's revenue mix, expense structure, surplus history, and implied endowment size — without a single phone call to the museum.

Every report includes a data confidence table — what was obtained, what remains partial, what requires direct outreach. Transparency about data quality is built into the system.
