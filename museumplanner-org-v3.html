The Convergence Era · 2026

The Museum Is No
Longer a Building.

It is a distributed interpretive system — an interconnected network of metadata, AI-driven content, sensors, and spatial computation. 30,000 American cities have civic buildings and no museum. That is about to change.

Museum Planner is where museum educators, exhibit designers, floor staff, and emerging professionals come to think about what comes next — and learn the tools to build it.
